Rural vs urban Mongolia on the gut microbiome

Abstract

The use of dairy products is ubiquitous in Mongolia as part of their dietary adaptation strategy. Consumption of dairy products enriches genus Bifidobacterium in the human gut. Working with Dr. Christina Warinner and Dr. Alexander Hüebner, we are investigating the impact of urbanization on gut microbiome composition of the Mongolian population in relation to lactase persistence using the MetaPhlAn and the MaAsLin2 packages.
