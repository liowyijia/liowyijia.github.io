Biography

Hello! I am a fellow at Harvard University after receiving my master’s from the University of Tokyo in 2022. My research focuses on the relationship between food culture, gut microbiota, and eating behavior.

Currently I am looking into the effects of Westernization on food preference through the route of gut-brain axis. In my master’s thesis, I investigated the effect of dietary fiber consumption on food preference mediated by the gut microbiota.

In Japan, I am affiliated with the Department of Human Ecology at the University of Tokyo and Laboratory of Microbiome Sciences at the RIKEN Center for Integrative Medical Sciences. At Harvard University, I am affiliated with the Nutrition & Microbial Ecology Laboratory in the Department of Human Evolutionary Biology.
